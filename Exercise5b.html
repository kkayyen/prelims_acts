Creamy Carbonara Recipe

Satisfy your cravings with this creamy, cheezy Carbonara.
It is now made simpler with this quick recipe! In just 30 minutes, you can make this delightful recipe.
Can make up to 4 servings. Stay happy, Stay healthy, Stay full!

Ingredients:

  • 1/4kg Spaghetti
  • ¾ cup (125g) Bacon
  • 6 Garlic
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 pack NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream
  • 1 sachet MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®
  • 1/8 tsp Freshly ground pepper
  • 4 tbsp Cheese
    •

Preparation:

  1. Boil pasta according to package direction. Do not over cook.
  2. While waiting for the pasta to be cooked, chop the bacon.
  3. Mince garlic.
  4. Grate cheese.
  5. Cook bacon in a pan to render fat. Strain bacon bits and set aside.

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic in the same pan until golden brown.
  2. Pour water and NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream.
  3. Stir in cheese and simmer until cheese is totally melted.
  4. Add cooked bacon.
  5. Season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP® and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss Spaghetti in sauce. Serve immediately.
